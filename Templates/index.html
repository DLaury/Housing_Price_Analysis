Welcome to House Price Analysis

About

We opened the doors of our website with a simple goal: to inspire and help people calculate the homes in Ames, Iowa

Ask a home buyer to describe their dream house, and they probably won't begin with the height of the basement ceiling or the proximity to an east-west railroad. But this model proves that much more influences price negotiations than the number of bedrooms or a white-picket fence. With 12 explanatory variables describing (almost) every aspect of residential homes in Ames, Iowa, this website helps you to predict the final price of each home.
